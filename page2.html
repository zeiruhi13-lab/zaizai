Nutrition Month 2026

Healthy Food, Healthy Life

About Nutrition Month

Nutrition Month is celebrated every July in the Philippines. It encourages everyone to eat healthy foods, practice good nutrition, and live an active lifestyle. A balanced diet helps people stay healthy, strong, and ready for everyday activities.

Benefits of Good Nutrition

  • Builds a strong and healthy body.
  • Provides energy throughout the day.
  • Strengthens the immune system.
  • Improves concentration and learning.
  • Helps prevent illnesses.

Healthy Foods to Eat

  1. Fresh Fruits
  2. Green Vegetables
  3. Fish
  4. Milk
  5. Whole Grains

Nutrition Month

"Healthy Food, Healthy Life"

What is Nutrition Month?

Nutrition Month is celebrated every July in the Philippines. It promotes healthy eating habits, proper nutrition, and a healthy lifestyle for everyone.

Importance of Good Nutrition

  • Helps our body grow strong.
  • Provides energy for daily activities.
  • Strengthens our immune system.
  • Improves learning and concentration.
  • Prevents diseases.

Healthy Foods

  1. Fruits
  2. Vegetables
  3. Fish
  4. Milk
  5. Whole grains

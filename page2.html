About Nutrition Month

Every July, the Philippines celebrates Nutrition Month to encourage healthy eating, proper nutrition, and an active lifestyle. It reminds everyone to choose nutritious foods and practice healthy habits.

Benefits of Good Nutrition

  • Builds a strong and healthy body.
  • Provides energy for daily activities.
  • Boosts the immune system.
  • Improves focus and learning.
  • Helps prevent illnesses.

Examples of Healthy Foods

  1. Fresh fruits
  2. Green vegetables
  3. Fish and other lean proteins
  4. Milk and dairy products
  5. Whole grains

Visit the National Nutrition Council website to learn more about healthy eating and nutrition.
